The blockchain has great potential to disrupt and improve a vast multitude of centralized services and organizations around the world.

VISION

We believe there is an important opportunity within the Ethereum network to create an exciting and unique platform for organizations to raise funds. Harnessing the trustless power of the smart contract we will create a way for people to both have a chance to win Ether and support the causes of their choice.

Every organization looking to raise funds from charities to podcasts to crowdfunding pages will be able to easily set up a method for donors to participate in a win / win situation. With no cost to the organization, added exposure and a huge boost of excitement, we see SPL/T as welcome addition to future fundraising campaigns.

ICO DETAILS

Token Name: SPL/T

What is the token? SPL/T tokens represent a Proof-of-Membership. The token grants access to a share of 50% of the fund growth quarterly. The remaining 50% is reinvested into the fund increasing the overall NAV.

Total Supply: 100 million

ICO Cap: 5 million USD
